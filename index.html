Meteor Sprint

Pilot a neon ship through a meteor storm. Dodge danger, collect stars, and chase a new high score.

Score 0
Best 0
Stars 0
Shields 3
Meteor Sprint requires a browser with canvas support.

Use Arrow keys or WASD to move. Press Space to start a run.

Objective

Stay alive as the storm intensifies. Meteors drain shields, stars boost your score, and every five stars repair one shield.

Controls

Move with Arrow keys or WASD. Press the button any time to restart, or press Space after a run ends to jump back in.

Tip

Sweep in short arcs instead of hugging one edge. Leaving yourself an escape route matters more than grabbing every star.
